Keyboard Controls:

D:Increase clockwise angle
A:Increase counter-clockwise angle
F:Increase Speed
S:Decrease Speed
+:Zoom in
-:Zoom out
Arrow keys:Panning left and right

Mouse Controls: (Preferred)
Hold the left mouse button, get the required angle using the mouse and release the mouse button to shoot. 
The distance from the mouse-coordinates to the center of the ball is used to calculate the initial velocities.
Use right mouse button to pan left and right.

Hitting the Brick: 1 point
Hitting the Pig: 2 points
